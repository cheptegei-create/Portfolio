Hydration of the Body

Always make sure that your body is always hydrated by trying to observe the doctors rule of taking eight glasses of water a day. It is also important to avoid drinking water before eating food because it will limit the amount of calories the body can absorb.

Eat lots of fruits

It is essential to eat fruits and drink fresh fruit juice more often. There is a saying in the medical field that states, "an apple a day, keeps the doctor away."

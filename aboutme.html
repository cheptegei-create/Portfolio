Job Experience

David did his student trainee program in the Ministry of mining in the year 2016. His main role in the ministry was the collection and storage of data collected from field explorations. In the year 2017, he got another trainee program in a building stone quarry at Makongeny where he acted as an assistant supervisor for maintenance of machinery utilized around the quarry. From the year 2018 to the current date, he started a gemstone dealership business and did freelance writing jobs as a part-time source of income.

Hence, it is the main reason why I chose to do programming.
